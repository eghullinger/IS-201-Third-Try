How to Make a String Hair Wrap

Instructions

  1. Step 1:
    • Take a small piece of hair and make a small braid.
    • Secure it with an elastic hair tie.
  2. Step 2:
    • Take three pieces of string, and cut them to be 4 times the length of the hair.
    • Fold the string in half so you now have six strings.
    • Tie a knot around the hair, so the string is secure around the braid.
  3. Step 3:
    • Pick a string to start with.
    • Hold all the other string against the braid, and wrap the chosen string around the other strings and the braid.
    • Make sure you are wrapping tightly. When ready to switch to the next string, drop the string you are using, and begin wrapping the braid with the next string.
    • Go until you have the desired pattern and have reached the end of the braid.
  4. Step 4:
    • When you have reached the end, remove the elastic from the braid, and resecure it again, this time including the ends of your string.
    • Cut off any excess.
    • To remove, take out the elastic and unwrap the braid until the knot slides out the top.
